What services does G7 Education offer?

G7 Education provides a range of tutoring services, including personalized tutoring, crash courses, group sessions, and more.

How can I book a tutoring session?

Booking a tutoring session is easy. Simply log in to your account, browse available tutors, choose a subject, and book a slot that suits your schedule.

Are there discounts for multiple sessions?

Yes, G7 Education offers discounts for multiple sessions.

Can I switch to a different tutor?

Yes, you can switch to a different tutor if you feel the need for a change. Visit your profile settings to explore available tutors and make the switch based on your preferences.

What subjects are covered on G7 Education?

G7 Education covers a wide range of subjects, including but not limited to mathematics, languages, chemistry, physics, programming, and humanities. Explore our platform to find the perfect tutor for your desired subject.

How do I resolve technical issues during a session?

If you encounter technical issues during a session, first, check your internet connection. If the problem persists, reach out to our support team through the contact page. We'll assist you in resolving any technical difficulties.
